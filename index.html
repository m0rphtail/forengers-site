Who Are We

While the majority of the population was comfortable being armchair judgmentals , playing the blame game and the being bearers of unsolicited and unfruitful advice, some of the more aware people, like us the Forengers decided to actually take some action.
“FORENGERS” is a group of likeminded people who come together with the motive of countering climate change, one step at a time. From a casual gathering of four people set out one breezy September morning to plant a sapling, to a community of 300+ youngsters gathering every Sunday morning – Forengers has grown into a social group which acts a host for each and every inspired youngster who wants to give back to the environment.

What We Do

Tree Plantation Drives

FORENGERS organizes plantation drives where we carry out tree plantations, tend to them, and undertake the necessary steps for their maintenance. Till date we have carried out 21 plantation drives in which we have planted above 250 saplings.

Cleanliness Drives

After the overwhelming success of their plantation drives , the FORENGERS took it upon themselves to organize cleanliness drives. These drives focus on a particular area which saturated waste and is cleaned by the vigilant group members with proper equipment and sanitation.

Paper Bag Making Drives

After COVID-19 hit us it was out of bounds to carry out plantation and cleanliness drives, but we refused to stop. We started making newspaper bags, cloth bags out of old waste t-shirts and newspaper envelopes for safe disposal of sanitary products.
