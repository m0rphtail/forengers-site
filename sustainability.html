Sustainability

Change is the only constant, but we aim to make a change that counts.
Our efforts do not perish with the changing weather. They stand strong against adversities as our change is in tandem with the environment.
We reuse and recycle the waste, to fuel our goals, for a better Tomorrow.


Green Shed Installation

We at forengers not only plant trees, but also take care of them after planting them. Green shed installation is one of the initiatives to help the plant grow more easily. The Green shed protects the plant from direct sunlight, strong winds and grazing animals. The green shed comprises of 3-4 dry bamboo sticks and a green cloth. Installing green sheds is fairly easy. It takes only 3-4 short bamboo sticks and green cloth of 4-5 feet length. We have successfully installed over 100 green sheds till now.
The installation of green sheds showed visible change in the quality of plant growth.

Tagging

We tag all of our saplings for better tracking and management of the saplings. All the saplings are first tagged and then mapped on paper for tracking them. We first used Cardboard cut-outs and Plastic pouches as shown in the photo for numbering the plants. But this method led to an increase in the plastic consumption that we starkly stand against. This led to the implementation of a new technique for tagging. We now use plastic bottle cut-outs made out of thrown away plastic bottles which are collected and kept aside in our cleanliness drives. This helps reusing the plastic bottles and avoids garbage. Tagging has helped us in the maintenance and for watering the plants.

Drip Irrigation

We install simple drip irrigation systems to the saplings for regulated water supply. The system is made of plastic bottles collected from our cleanliness drives which promotes reuse of the plastic bottles. This system facilitates the plants to with a continous stream water even when no one is available to water the plants. This easy to make drip system only takes one bottle and one bamboo stick for support makinf it highly effecient. We have installed more than 120 drip irrigation systems till date.
These drip systems keep our plants hydrated and healthy with minimum dependence on us.

Watering Drive

Forengers is one of the very few organizations who take care of the plants even after planting it. We arrange 4 watering drives each week. These watering drives are fundamental towards the maintenance of the saplings.

Record Of Growth

After the sapling is planted, we record its growth every week at our drives. The status of the saplings health is taken into account. We also keep track of the height of the saplings regularly. This helps in planning and allocation new plantations and to take care of saplings which are already planted. Keeping tabs on the plants contribute towards the longevity of the plantation.
