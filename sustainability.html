Green Shed Installation

We at forengers not only plant trees, but also take care of them after planting them. Green shed installation is one of the initiatives to help the plant grow more easily. The Green shed installation protects the plant from direct sunlight and strong winds. It also protects the sapling from grazing animals. The green shed comprises of 3-4 dry bamboo sticks and a green cloth. Installing green sheds is fairly easy. It takes only 3-4 short bamboo sticks and green cloth of 4-5 feet length. We have successfully installed over 100 green sheds till now. We noticed a considerable improvement in the growth of plants after installing the sheds.

Tagging

We tag all of our saplings for better tracking and management of the saplings. All the saplings are first tagged and then mapped on paper for tracking them. We first used Cardboard cut-outs and Plastic pouches as shown in the photo for numbering the plants. But that way was not eco friendly as it was using plastic both cardboard and plastic pouches. But now weâ€™ve implemented a new technique for tagging. We now use plastic bottle cut-outs made out of thrown away plastic bottles which are collected and kept aside in our cleanliness drives. This helps reusing the plastic bottles and avoids garbage. Tagging has helped us in the maintenance and for watering the plants.

Drip Irrigation

We also install simple drip irrigation systems to the saplings for regulated water supply to them. The system is again made of plastic bottles collected from our cleanliness drives which promotes reuse of the plastic bottles. This system also helps the plants to water when no one is available to water the plants. The system only takes one bottle and one bamboo stick for support which also is a fairly easy method to install the system. We have installed more than 120 drip irrigation systems till date.

Watering Drive

Forengers is one of the very few organizations who takes care of the plants even after planting it. We arrange weekly watering drives which take place 4 times in a week. All of our plants are taken good care of.

Record Of Growth

After the sapling is planted, we record its growth every week at our drives. The status of the sapling like alive, healthy, dead, needs attention is noted in a notebook. We also keep track of the height of the saplings regularly. This helps in any new plantations and to take care of saplings which are already planted.
